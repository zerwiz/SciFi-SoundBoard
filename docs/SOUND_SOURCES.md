## Download tips

1. **Freesound** – Create a free account to download. Filter by license: “Creative Commons 0” or “CC-BY” for commercial use.
2. **BigSoundBank** – No login for many files; CC0, so no attribution required.
3. **Pixabay / ZapSplat** – Free account may be required; check “free for commercial use.”
4. **Rename after download** to match the app ID (e.g. `blaster.mp3`, `red_alert.mp3`) and place in `public/sounds/` (or your app’s assets folder).
5. **Convert to MP3** if you only get WAV (e.g. with ffmpeg or an online converter) to keep bundle size smaller.
